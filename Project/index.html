About Us

KingSukh is a boutique guest house offering personalized service and convenient city access. We focus on clean rooms, helpful staff, and value for money.

Our Mission

To provide comfortable stays and memorable experiences to our guests.

Location

Central location with easy access to transport and local attractions.
